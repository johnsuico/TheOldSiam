The Old Siam

The Old Siam is an authentic Thai restaurant locationed in Sunnyvale, CA on El Camino Real between Mary Ave. and S. Bernaldo Ave.


We offer a wide variety of authentic Thai dishes which are made to order.


Our number one priority is cleanliness and freshness of food. We focus on making sure our dishes are as authentic as possible based on traditional Thai recipes.
